Overview

Purpose

My goal is to make a semi-functional website for a fictional car rental company. The home page won't have much, the bulk of it will be on the search page with the actual car info.

Audience

Anyone looking to rent a car!

Dynamic elements

I want a simple search bar to be able to look up specific car brands. I also think it would be cool to have a simple form to fill out that lets you reserve a rental.
